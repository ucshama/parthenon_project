The frieze on the east side of the [Parthenon](https://www.theacropolismuseum.gr/en/parthenon-east-frieze-block-2-ii#:~:text=The%20frieze%20on%20the%20east,the%20Temple%20of%20Athena%20Polias.) shows the arrival on the Acropolis of the procession formed by the people of Athens during the festival of the Panathenaia in honour of the protectress of the city, Athena.

Section 1:
With a gesture, an atholetes or games steward provides the link between the north and east sides of the frieze. The procession continues.
<img src="{{ site.baseurl }}/eastfriezepics/east1.jpeg" width="100%" height="50">

Section 2:
On the east side the two sections of the procession come together. Only here are women shown participating.
